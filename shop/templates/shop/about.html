The Tradition

Not Just a Pickle. An Emotion.

At Mami's, we believe that a meal is incomplete without that tiny burst of flavor on the side of your plate. What started as a small kitchen experiment in Ramanattukara has now grown into a passion project to bring authentic, preservative-free flavors to your dining table.

Our Promise: We don't use factory machines. Every mango is cut by hand, every lemon is sun-dried, and our spices are roasted in small batches to ensure the aroma stays locked in.

We use only pure Gingelly Oil and high-grade spices. No artificial colors. No dangerous chemicals. Just the taste of home, preserved in a jar.

- With Love, Mami.
